NEXUS 2023, Kongu Engineering College, National Level Technical Symposium, Erode, Tamil Nadu, 6th April 2023

About the event

Nexus is an Inter-collegiate technical symposium organized with pomp and grandeur every year by the Department of Mechatronics Engineering, Kongu Engineering College. Nexus provides a huge platform for students all around the nation to portray their talents and showcase their skills through jam-packed webinars and various events. The event will be held on 6th April 2023.
